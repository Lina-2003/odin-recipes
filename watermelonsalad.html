Watermelon Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 large watermelon
  • 2 cucumbers
  • 12 leaves fresh mint
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tbls apple cider vinegar
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese

Directions

  1. Wash your hands
  2. Cut watermelon into 1-inch cubes
  3. Cut cucumbers into thin slices
  4. Mix watermelon, cucumber and mint in a large bowl
  5. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, salt and pepper in a small bowl
  6. Drizzle over salad and add feta cheese on top
