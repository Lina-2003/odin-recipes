Patty

Patty for those who want a high in calories meal, and minimal amounts of carbs

Ingredients

  • 350g meat
  • cheese
  • 1 avocado
  • pepper
  • salt
  • mustard
  • ketchup

Directions

  1. Wash your hands
  2. Form a patty with your hands
  3. Cook the meat on medium heat
  4. Season the patty while it's still raw
  5. Mash avocado and season it with salt and pepper
  6. Flip the patty and add the avocado, ketchup and mustard, and lastly the cheese slices on top
  7. Turn down the heat and cover the frying pan with a lid and let the cheese melt over the patty
