Tacos

Delicious tacos perfect for gathering the family together saturday night

Ingredients

  • tortillas
  • 800g meat
  • cucumber
  • tomato
  • pepper bells
  • onion
  • cheese
  • sour cream
  • taco sauce

Directions

  1. Wash your hands
  2. Cook the meat on medium heat
  3. Chop up the onion, cucumber, tomato and the pepper bells
  4. Heat up the tortillas
  5. Voila! add sour cream, cheese and taco sauce to your mix and you're done!
